### Connectedness

Theorem 1 Let $G$ be a connected graph with diameter $\delta$. Then $F_{k}(G)$ is connected with diameter at least $k(\delta-k+1)$ and at most $k \delta$.

    Proof of the thing

If $G$ is $t$-connected, then $F_{k}(G)$ is $t$-connected for all $k \geq 1$.
